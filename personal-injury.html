Personal Injury

During the first quarter of 2021 I was involved in a car accident. My vehicle was hit from behind on my way home from work at a high rate of speed by a distracted driver. The experience left my mind with emotions from fear to anger to disappointment while anxiety + hypervigilance arrived & lingered thereafter. With my mind scared, my body was simultaneously attempting to heal from whiplash, hearing loss, and soft tissue damage.

As a result, I now dedicate a portion of my practice to providing assessment, therapy (counseling), and providing assessment results + documentation to primary providers in efforts to support your coordination of care. I accept direct inquiry, medical provider, or attorney of record referrals to ensure you get quality therapy services.

Personal Injury cases are done via lien and treatment is centered around injury from accident. Treatment is brief in nature with the goal of pre-accident function not withstanding prior mental health challenges.

Do you require my services?

Things to consider:

  1. Has your mood changed since the accident?
  2. Do you find yourself on-guard or more easily startled since the accident?
  3. Are you struggling to manage injuries sustained because of the accident?
  4. Do you find navigating the process (injury, claim, multiple providers) overly stressful or upsetting and need help processing it?

Accidents happen. So does support & validation for your experience. Your feelings, thoughts, and emotions after a no-fault accident often benefit from talking about it in a safe space.

Be well.
